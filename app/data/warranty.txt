Standard Warranty Policy

1. Warranty Duration:
   The product is covered under warranty for 12 months from the date of purchase.

2. What is Covered:
   - Manufacturing defects
   - Hardware malfunctions not caused by user damage
   - Battery failure under normal usage conditions

3. What is NOT Covered:
   - Physical damage (cracks, dents, broken screens)
   - Water or liquid damage
   - Damage caused by mishandling or accidents
   - Unauthorized repairs or modifications

4. Replacement Policy:
   - Replacement is applicable only within 7 days of delivery
   - Product must be in original condition with no physical damage

5. Disclaimer:
   Final warranty or replacement approval is subject to manual inspection by the support team.
